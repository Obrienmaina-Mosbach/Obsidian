Travel Desitinaton

Bora Bora is a small island in the South Pacific, part of French Polynesia, known for its stunning turquoise lagoon, luxurious overwater bungalows, and breathtaking natural beauty. It is located about 230 km (143 miles) northwest of Tahiti and is surrounded by a barrier reef and small islets, creating a calm, crystal-clear lagoon.

  • Key Facts about Bora Bora
  • Country: French Polynesia (an overseas collectivity of France)
  • Language: French and Tahitian (English is widely spoken in tourist areas)
  • Currency: CFP Franc (XPF)
  • Main Island: 29.3 km² (11.3 sq miles)
  • Population: ~10,000 people
  • Time Zone: Tahiti Time (UTC -10)
